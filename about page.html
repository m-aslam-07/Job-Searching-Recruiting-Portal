Our Story

Founded with a vision to transform the job market, Off Campus bridges the gap between talented professionals and innovative companies.

In 2018, our founders experienced firsthand the frustrations of the traditional job search process. Job seekers faced endless applications with little feedback, while employers struggled to find qualified candidates in a sea of resumes.

We set out to create a platform that values quality over quantity, using smart technology to match the right talent with the right opportunities. Today, Off Campus serves over 1 million users and has facilitated more than 50,000 successful career placements.

Our mission remains the same: to make the job search and hiring process more efficient, transparent, and rewarding for everyone involved.
