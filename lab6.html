Science Symposium 2025

Event Description

Welcome to the annual Science Symposium hosted by the Department of Physics and Life Sciences. This event brings together students, researchers, and experts to explore the latest advancements in scientific research and innovation. Join us for a day of enlightening presentations, interactive workshops, and networking opportunities.

Topics Covered

  • Renewable Energy Technologies
  • Biotechnology and Health
  • Quantum Physics and Computing
  • Environmental Sustainability
  • Artificial Intelligence Applications

Guest Speakers

  • Dr. Asha Verma – Environmental Scientist
  • Prof. Rajesh Kumar – Quantum Computing Expert
  • Dr. Meera Singh – Biotechnology Researcher
